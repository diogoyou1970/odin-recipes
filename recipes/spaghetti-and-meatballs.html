Delicious and easy spaghetti and meatballs recipe

Make an incredible spaghetti and meatballs with tomato and garlic sauce in just 20 minutes for you and your family!

Ingredients

  • Pork mixed or cow meatballs
  • Spaghetti
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Tomato pulp
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic very finely, and cut the onion into cube-sized pieces (about the size of your nail)
  2. Heat olive oil and fry the meatballs until they are browned (season with salt and pepper)
  3. Remove the meatballs to a plate (don't remove the olive oil)
  4. First, add the onion, and after about a minute, add the garlic
  5. Once the onion is translucent, add a bit of tomato paste and mix it with the onion
  6. Add chopped tomatoes to the pan and mix, mashing them so they break down
  7. Add the meatballs back into the pan and mix with the other ingredients. Add tomato pulp and any other seasonings, stir, and let it boil
